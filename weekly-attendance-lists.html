Regpack Weekly Attendance Lists daily + weekly

Import a Regpack CSV export filtered to ordered and locked weekly and/or daily registrations. The app totals attendance for each week and creates alphabetized printable lists with registered days marked.

Daily registrations are grouped into weeks using the “Week 1 Monday” date below. Everything runs locally in your browser; CSV files are not uploaded anywhere.
