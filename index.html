Manager Tools

Browser-based tools for processing Regpack CSV exports.

T-Shirt Counter

Upload Regpack CSV exports and total Youth Small, Medium, and Large shirt quantities.

First-Week File List

Upload summer registration exports and list each child under the first week they attend.

Weekly Attendance Lists

Upload locked weekly registration exports and print alphabetized attendance lists for each week.
Privacy note: these tools run in your browser. CSV files are read locally on your device by the page and are not uploaded to a server.
